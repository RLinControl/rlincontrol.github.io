Seminars

  • Overview: As part of the course, students are expected to participate in two seminar sessions. These seminars aim to deepen understanding of the tools and platforms used in Reinforcement Learning (RL) research and applications.

Seminar 1: RL Simulation Platforms (0.75 out of 20)

  • Format: Team-based (2 members per team). Each team selects and presents one RL platform.
  • Requirements:
    • Full introduction of the chosen simulation platform.
    • Demonstration of sample simulation results.
    • Emphasis on clarity, completeness, and technical depth.
  • Example Platforms: Gymnasium, PettingZoo, RLlib, Dopamine, IsaacSIM (...and more in the list below)

Seminar 2: General RL Methods (0.75 out of 20)

  • Format: Team-based (2 members per team). Each team selects one general or trending RL method to present.
  • Requirements:
    • Clear and comprehensive presentation of the selected topic.
    • No implementation is required.
    • Focus on motivations, challenges, and modern trends in RL research.
  • Recommendation: Choose trending or impactful topics. Use structured slides and visual explanations where helpful.
